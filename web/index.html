About GCode Viewer

This is a viewer for GCode files. GCode files are set of commands sent to CNC machines such as a RepRap or MakerBot 3D printer.

This viewer shows the operations the machine will take.

Drag the mouse to rotate the model. Hold down 'S' to zoom.
